The Mandalorian

Olarom – Welcome!

This website delves into the world of Star Wars, with a specific focus on The Mandalorian TV show and the developing Mandalorian culture within the Star Wars galaxy.

You will be able to learn about the evolution of the Mandalorians from the official introduction of Boba Fett in the Star Wars film, The Empire Strikes Back (1980), to the latest stories found in the newest Disney Plus show, The Book of Boba Fett.

An expanding story developed around ‘the bounty hunter in the Mandalorian armour’ in the 1980s, spawning numerous novels in the time between the creation of further Star Wars films and TV shows, and that later developed into the establishment of the Taung race, who became the progenitors of the Mandalorian culture in the Star Wars canon.

The evolution of the Mandalorian culture and the diverse characters that sprang from it would lead to the development of a language named Mando’a. Mando’a would feature heavily in many Star Wars games and book series, later being used in shows such as The Clone Wars, Rebels, and the latest season of The Book of Boba Fett.

To learn more, click on the Beskaryc Kar'ta or Iron Heart, which is the centrepiece of Mandalorian Armour and the object that will allow users to navigate their way through the mythos of the Mandalorians!
